Introduction of Project

Each student was assigned a scholarly journal related to digital rhetoric. The students were to become "word watcher[s]" (Mueller, p. 73) and conduct a distant reading of the last five published issues. They then created a series of word clouds to share and discuss with the digital rhetoric community, and were previously publish as a webtext on their profile/portfolio sites

Journals to be discussed: Kairos, Encultration, XChanges, Hybrid Pedagogy, Digital Humanities Quarterly, Present Tense, Journal of Multimodal Rhetorics, Peitho, and Technoculture
